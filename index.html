Welcome to emspectrum.info

We’re creating a way for you to understand the Electromagnetic Spectrum.

Once 100 people like you have registered their interest we will build the only Visual Tour of the Electromagnetic Spectrum — and you will be the first to get it, absolutely free.


Why give you my email address?

Your email address tells us that this is something worth building; that other people want to understand the electromagnetic spectrum — and, it’s how we’ll contact you when it’s ready.

Emspectrum.info will be built by developers of Automathic.org, Algebrarules.com, and The Free & Open Science Class (as well a number of other sites) — two autodidacts with a passion for science.

Who is this for?

Everybody. The curious, the intelligent, scientists and non-scientists, students and professionals: anyone who wants to understand the world they live in — You.
